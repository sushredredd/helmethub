06 — Why trust us

Certification, decoded

ISI (IS 4151)

India's legal minimum. Every helmet we list carries a genuine ISI mark or exceeds it with international ratings.

DOT (FMVSS 218)

The US standard — higher-energy impact testing. A useful step up on budget and mid-range lids.

ECE 22.06

Europe's current benchmark, adding rotational and oblique-impact tests. The gold standard we recommend for highway riders.

Fit beats features

A well-fitting ₹2,000 helmet protects better than a loose ₹20,000 one. Our guides always start with fit.
