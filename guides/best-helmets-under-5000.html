Frequently asked questions

Axor Apex or LS2 FF352?

Apex wins on ventilation and aggression; the Rookie wins on liner comfort and weight. Both are excellent — fit should decide.

Is ECE 22.05 still okay in 2026?

Yes. 22.06 is the newer, stricter standard, but a genuine 22.05 helmet remains highly protective. Prefer 22.06 when the price is close.

Do I need Pinlock?

If you ride in monsoon or early mornings, absolutely. A Pinlock-ready visor lets you add the anti-fog insert later.
