Frequently asked questions

ADV helmet vs motocross helmet?

MX lids need goggles and vent maximally for low-speed technical riding. ADV lids add a face shield for highway comfort — the right pick for touring.

Do peaks work on highways?

Above ~90 km/h peaks add buffeting. Most ADV peaks remove with three screws for big highway days.

Top pick overall?

RE Escapade for Himalayan owners; Axxis Gecko SV for the most complete feature set.
