Frequently asked questions

Do I really need a peak?

On trails, yes — it blocks sun, roost and branches. On long highway stretches it adds buffeting; removable peaks give both worlds.

Goggles or visor for Ladakh?

Carry both. Visor for highway days, goggles for dust sections — ADV lids with goggle-ready ports let you switch.

Best value ADV pick?

Ignyte IGN-11 Trail under ₹4.5K, or the Axor X-Cross for the full triple-visor kit under ₹6K.
