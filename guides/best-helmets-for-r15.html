Frequently asked questions

Why does upward vision matter on an R15?

In the sport crouch you look 'up' through the top of the eye port. Wide-viewport shells like the LS2 Rapid work best.

Ideal weight?

Under 1,450 g keeps neck strain down in a sporty posture, especially in traffic.

Budget pick?

Ignyte IGN-4 or Axor Rage RR3 give sporty shells with dual certification under ₹4.5K.
