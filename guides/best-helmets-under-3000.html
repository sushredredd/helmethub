Frequently asked questions

Is DOT better than ISI?

They test differently. DOT (US) involves higher-energy impacts; ISI is India's legal requirement. A dual ISI+DOT helmet has passed both — a good sign at this price.

Can I tour on a ₹3,000 helmet?

Occasionally, yes. For regular highway riding, step up to an ECE-rated helmet with better aero and noise control.

What about ventilation?

Look for real intake + exhaust vents. Budget shells often have cosmetic vents that move no air.
