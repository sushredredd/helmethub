Frequently asked questions

Is an ISI helmet under ₹1,500 actually safe?

A genuine ISI-marked helmet meets IS 4151, India's legal minimum, and is vastly safer than the ₹300 roadside caps. Fit matters as much as certification — buy your correct size.

Should I buy a full face or flip-up at this budget?

Full face gives maximum chin protection. Flip-ups like the Studds Ninja Elite trade a little protection for traffic convenience. Avoid half helmets entirely.

How long does a budget helmet last?

Replace after any impact, or every 3–5 years as EPS foam and padding degrade — sooner if used daily in Indian summers.
