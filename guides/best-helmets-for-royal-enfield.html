Frequently asked questions

Open face or full face on a Classic 350?

Full face is always safer. If you choose open-face for the vibe, buy fiberglass with DOT/ECE rating and accept the trade-off consciously.

Are Royal Enfield branded helmets good?

Yes — they're properly certified (several are ECE 22.06 fiberglass) and priced fairly for the build quality.

Best pick for an Interceptor 650?

The higher speeds deserve a full-face: RE Streetwind Pro or Axor Apex-class lids suit the 650 twins.
