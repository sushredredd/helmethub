Frequently asked questions

What's the single best feature to look for under ₹2,000?

An inner sun visor. It transforms daily riding in Indian sun, and models like the Studds Thunder D1 offer it well under budget.

Are flip-up helmets legal in India?

Yes, if ISI marked. Ride with the chin bar closed — an open flip-up protects like an open-face helmet.

Vega or Studds at this price?

Both are credible. Vega tends to fit slightly rounder heads, Studds slightly oval — try both if you can.
